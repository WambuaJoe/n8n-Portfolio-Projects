About This Workflow

This project streamlines the process of capturing leads from forms or landing pages and enriching them with additional data before adding them to a CRM. Once a lead is captured, the workflow automatically pulls in company info, social profiles, and other public data from enrichment APIs. The enriched profile is then pushed to a CRM like HubSpot, Pipedrive, or Salesforce. The automation ensures that sales teams always have a complete and accurate lead record without manual research. It reduces response time, increases personalization, and boosts conversion rates.

Tech Used

  • n8n
  • Webhook or Form Trigger Node
  • Airtable
  • Clearbit/FullContact API (Lead Enrichment)
